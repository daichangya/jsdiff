Privacy Policy for JSDiff

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

JSDiff ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our JSON diff tool at jsdiff.com.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

JSDiff is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect or store:

  • Any JSON data, code, or text you input into the tool
  • Personal identification information
  • Uploaded files or their contents
  • Comparison results or diff outputs

3. Local Processing

All data processing occurs entirely within your web browser. Your data never leaves your device or is transmitted to our servers. This ensures complete privacy and security of your sensitive information.

4. Analytics

We use basic analytics tools (Google Analytics and Baidu Analytics) to understand website usage patterns. These tools collect anonymous data such as:

  • Page views and visit duration
  • Browser type and device information
  • Referring websites

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual users.

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies solely for analytics purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as GitHub). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

7. Children's Privacy

JSDiff does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
