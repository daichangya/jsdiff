JSDiff vs Diffy – Why We Don't Compare

Diffy (diffy.website) is a visual regression testing tool: it compares website screenshots and UI changes, often used in CI/CD. It is not a JSON or text diff tool. JSDiff is a JSON/JavaScript text comparison tool. Because they serve different purposes, we no longer compare them side by side.
