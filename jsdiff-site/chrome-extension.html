How to Install

Visit Chrome Web Store

Click the "Add to Chrome" button above to go to the Chrome Web Store.

Click "Add to Chrome"

Click the blue "Add to Chrome" button in the top-right corner of the page.

Confirm Installation

Click "Add extension" in the popup dialog to confirm the installation.

Start Using

Click the JSDiff icon in your browser toolbar to start comparing files offline.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the extension free?

Yes! The JSDiff Chrome Extension is completely free to use, just like our online tool.

Does it work offline?

Yes! Once installed, the extension works completely offline. No internet connection required.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally on your computer. Your data is never sent to any server.

How do I update the extension?

Chrome automatically updates extensions. You'll always have the latest version.
